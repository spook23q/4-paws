Account & Data Deletion

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

At 4 Paws, we respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your account and all associated data from our platform.

Delete Your Account In-App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly through the 4 Paws app:

  1. 1Open the 4 Paws app and sign in to your account
  2. 2Go to the Profile tab (bottom navigation bar)
  3. 3Scroll down to find the "Delete Account" button (red button at the bottom)
  4. 4Enter your password to verify your identity
  5. 5Type "DELETE" in the confirmation field
  6. 6Tap "Delete My Account" to permanently remove your account

Immediate Deletion

When you delete your account through the app, your data is removed immediately from our active systems. This is the fastest method for account deletion.

Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via email, you can contact us directly:

Request Account Deletion via Email

Please include the following information in your request:

  • The email address associated with your 4 Paws account
  • Your full name as it appears on your profile
  • Confirmation that you want to delete your account and all associated data

We will process email deletion requests within 7 business days and send you a confirmation once completed.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and password
  • Profile Data: Profile photos, bio, and preferences
  • Pet Information: All cat profiles you have created
  • Booking History: All past and pending bookings
  • Messages: All conversations with other users
  • Reviews: Reviews you have written and received
  • Payment Information: Any stored payment methods (processed through Stripe)
  • Push Notification Tokens: Device tokens for notifications

Important: This Action Cannot Be Undone

Account deletion is permanent. Once your account is deleted, you will not be able to recover any of your data, including booking history, messages, or reviews. If you wish to use 4 Paws again in the future, you will need to create a new account.

Data Retention After Deletion

After account deletion, we may retain certain information for the following purposes:

  • Legal Compliance: Transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years for tax and legal purposes
  • Fraud Prevention: Basic account identifiers may be retained for up to 3 years to prevent fraud
  • Dispute Resolution: If there are pending disputes or chargebacks, relevant data may be retained until resolved

Any retained data is stored securely and is not used for marketing or shared with third parties except as required by law.

Third-Party Data

Please note that deleting your 4 Paws account does not automatically delete data held by third-party services:

  • Stripe: Payment history may be retained by Stripe according to their data retention policy. Visit Stripe's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Expo: Push notification tokens are automatically invalidated when your account is deleted.
