Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

Welcome to 4 Paws ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Profile photos, bio, and preferences for cat owners and sitters
  • Pet Information: Your cat's name, breed, age, medical conditions, and special care requirements
  • Location Data: Your address and real-time location for matching with nearby sitters and geofencing features
  • Payment Information: Credit card details and billing address (processed securely through Stripe)
  • Communications: Messages exchanged between cat owners and sitters through our platform
  • Reviews and Ratings: Feedback you provide about sitters or cat owners

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Match cat owners with qualified sitters in their area
  • Process bookings and payments
  • Enable real-time messaging between users
  • Send push notifications about booking updates and messages
  • Provide geofencing alerts for cat safety monitoring
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your profile information is visible to potential matches (sitters see owner profiles and vice versa)
  • Service Providers: We share data with third-party services that help us operate our platform (payment processing, cloud hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-Out: Disable push notifications in your device settings
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@4paws.app.

6. Location Services

Our app uses location services for:

  • Finding nearby cat sitters
  • Geofencing alerts to monitor your cat's safety during sitting sessions
  • Verifying sitter check-ins at your home

You can disable location services in your device settings, but some features may not function properly without location access.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with third-party services including:

  • Stripe: For secure payment processing
  • Expo: For push notifications
  • Google Maps: For location and mapping features

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account deletion, we may retain certain information for legal compliance, dispute resolution, or fraud prevention for up to 3 years.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice in the app or sending you an email. Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
